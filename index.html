The machine’s humanization has begun a more and more truth in our days. Today, we can see that our cellphones project us. In a sense that they take our personalities, our preferences and can obey only to our voices. We sit in a world where this little devices took our place, we are represented by them on our social media, they know our music taste, the clothes we want to buy, the movies we prefer, all these because they are linked to us, these “personas”. Busines and companies want our attention - That’s their product, so they look for the devices that projects the specific type of people they want, connect to them and have the perfect target for their business.
